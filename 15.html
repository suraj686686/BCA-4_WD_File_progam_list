The Department of Computer Science offers programs of study leading to the Bachelor of Science, Master of Science, and Doctor of Philosophy degrees in computer science.

About Us

The Department of Computer Science at Suraj University has a long history of excellence in research and education. Our faculty members are world-renowned experts in their fields, and our graduates go on to successful careers in industry and academia.
